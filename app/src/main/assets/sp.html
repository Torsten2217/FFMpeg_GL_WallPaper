About ScreensPro

ScreensPro is a company dedicated to providing the highest quality, most diverse animated wallpapers and screensavers for Android devices and computers. For more information about the company, please visit www.screenspro.com

The ScreensPro Android app is a library of downloadable looped videos that can be set as Live Wallpapers on both phone and tablet devices.

Giving 10% back to the planet!

Here at ScreensPro we love nature and especially animals. We love to film nature and have been lucky enough to travel around the world, seeing the beauty that it has to show... Unfortunately, we have also seen the problems that the planet is battling.

At the end of each business year, we will give 10% of our profits to a selection of charities that try to help keep our planet healthy and help endangered wildlife that are on the brink of extinction. This is not an empty promise and the more money we make, the more we will increase the % given to charity.
